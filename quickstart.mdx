## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- A valid email address for account verification
- No prior installation required - everything runs in your browser

</Callout>

## Sign Up for Your McCartney Account

Creating an account takes less than a minute. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://mccartney.com/signup` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from McCartney and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://mccartney.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

A documentation space organizes all docs for a single project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select the `New Space` button in the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and add an optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Visibility" icon="globe">
    Select `Public` for sharing or `Private` for team access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Space" icon="check-circle">
    Click `Create` to generate your space. It appears in your dashboard list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Spaces support custom domains, branding with your color `#3B82F6`, and collaborative editing.

</Callout>

## Add Your First Project Document

Inside your new space, create a document using Markdown or the visual editor.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Editor" icon="code">
    Use the Markdown editor for full control.

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: My First Doc
    description: Welcome to my project.
    ---

    ## Overview

    This is your first document in McCartney.
    ````

    ````markdown
    # My First Doc

    ## Overview

    This is your first document in McCartney.
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Visual Editor" icon="type">
    Drag-and-drop components like cards and steps without writing code. Preview changes live as you build.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

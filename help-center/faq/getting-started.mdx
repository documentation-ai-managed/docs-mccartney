## Getting Started

To begin documenting your project in mccartney:

1. Log in to your mccartney dashboard.
2. Click the **New Page** button in the left sidebar.
3. Enter a title and select a documentation category.
4. Use the editor to add your content (Markdown supported).
5. Click **Publish** when you are ready.

After publishing, your page will appear in the documentation list and be visible to your team.

## Overview

Categories help organize your mccartney documentation.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Categories">
    In the dashboard sidebar, click **Categories**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new category">
    Click **Add Category**, then enter a name (e.g. "API Docs").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and use">
    Click **Save**. You can now assign documentation pages to this category.
  </Step>
</Steps>
